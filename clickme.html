Personnas
Gamer (students)
Visits CM from his phone at school or home. He like sexy and ACG categories and jump directly to these catagories to check if new articles have been uploaded.
Pain point: Sexy articles are splited into many categories. He would like to acces this content quicklier.
Procrastinator (secretry)
Often visits CM at work. She doesn’t really know what she looks for, she just wants to read interesting articles. When she likes them she shares on her facebook.
Pain point: When she reads an article sometime she got some “sexy” articles propositions with subjectives pictures on the right side. When it’s happen when she is in a public space she immediatly closes the window.
